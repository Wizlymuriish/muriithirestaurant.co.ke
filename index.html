Muriithi’s Restaurant is a premier dining destination located in the heart of the city, offering a perfect blend of tradition, flavor, and hospitality. Known for its elegant ambiance and warm service, the restaurant caters to food lovers who crave authentic local and international cuisine in a refined setting. With a spacious, beautifully designed interior and street-facing views, Muriithi’s Restaurant stands tall as a culinary landmark. The menu features signature dishes like spicy dum biryani, chicken chow mein, ugali with goat wet fry, buffalo chicken wings, and aromatic pilau — all freshly prepared using high-quality ingredients. Whether you're stopping by for a casual lunch, a business meeting, or a family dinner, Muriithi’s delivers an unforgettable experience.
